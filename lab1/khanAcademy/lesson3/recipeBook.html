Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Eggs 2

Step 1: Mix flour, milk, and eggs in a bowl until smooth.

Step 2: Heat a non-stick pan and pour batter to form pancakes. Flip when bubbles form.

Step 3: Serve warm with syrup and butter.

Source: Fluffy Pancakes Recipe

Greek Salad

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Cucumber 1
Tomatoes 2
Feta Cheese 100g

Step 1: Chop the vegetables and place them in a bowl.

Step 2: Add crumbled feta cheese and olives if desired.

Step 3: Drizzle with olive oil and a pinch of salt. Toss and serve.
